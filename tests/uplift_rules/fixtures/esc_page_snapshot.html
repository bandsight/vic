Annual council rate caps

The 2025-26 rate cap is 3.00 per cent.

This cap applies to all Victorian councils from 1 July 2025.

Approved higher rate caps

Brimbank City Council has an approved higher cap of 3.50 per cent for 2025-26.

Melton City Council has an approved higher cap of 3.25 per cent for 2025-26.

Historical caps

The 2024-25 rate cap was 2.75 per cent.

The 2023-24 rate cap was 3.50 per cent.
